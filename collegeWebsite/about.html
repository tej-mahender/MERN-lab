Prasad V Potluri Siddhartha Institute of Technology (PVPSIT), established in 1998, is a pioneering self-financed Institution renowned for Academic Excellence and Discipline. The college is sponsored by Siddhartha Academy of General and Technical Education, the premier Organization that runs 18 Educational Institutions in diverse fields of study.

The college offers the following academic programs:

  • B.Tech in Computer Science Engineering (CSE)
  • B.Tech in CSE - Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (CSM)
  • B.Tech in CSE - Data Science (CSD)
  • B.Tech in Information Technology (IT)
  • B.Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)
  • B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering (ME)
  • B.Tech in Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE)
  • B.Tech in Civil Engineering (CE)
  • M.Tech in ECE
  • M.Tech in ME
  • MBA

PVPSIT is located at Kanuru in Vijayawada, well-connected by Rail, Road, and Air. Situated on a sprawling 19.98-acre campus, the college boasts state-of-the-art infrastructure with well-equipped laboratories housed in a Monolithic RCC Structure spanning a built-up area of 36,537 square meters.

The Institution is Autonomous, approved by AICTE, permanently affiliated with JNTUK, Kakinada, and accredited by NAAC with an A+ grade. All UG programs are accredited by NBA. PVPSIT is also ISO 9001:2015 certified and widely acclaimed for its quality standards. UGC has accorded the Institution 2f/12B Status, and the Government of Andhra Pradesh has conferred an A grade. The college is ranked in the 101–150 band in the NIRF Innovation stream.

The Institution provides ample facilities for research, innovation, and entrepreneurial activities. PVPSIT has MOUs with over 60 reputed industries, ensuring students are exposed to industry-relevant learning. The placement cell offers career guidance and enables students to secure placements, with the highest pay package reaching ₹44 LPA.

PVPSIT’s curriculum is designed to meet industrial needs and is periodically revised. The institution has introduced a Choice Based Credit System from PVP20 Regulations, offering students opportunities to pursue Honors and Minors. PVPSIT alumni are well-settled in India and abroad, maintaining strong ties with the institution and sharing knowledge on cutting-edge technologies.

Committed to holistic development, PVPSIT constantly reminds students of their social responsibilities. The college promotes participation in co-curricular and extra-curricular activities, providing facilities for indoor and outdoor games, Yoga, NSS, and NCC activities. The Women's Cell fosters gender equity, while the Students Council enables active student participation in academic and administrative matters.
